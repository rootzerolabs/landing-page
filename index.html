The problem with generic AI

Off-the-shelf AI doesn't know your matters — and learns nothing by helping.

General assistants sit next to a lawyer's work. They can't see the deposition, the site logs, or last week's partner note — so they guess, and guesses are malpractice risk.

LawnchAI absorbs the work instead. Every document, note, intake form and decision enters your firm's private retrieval index — context that grounds the next answer — and the model that learns it is dedicated to your firm, never pooled, never used to train anything outside your walls.

Grounded, not guessing

Every answer cites the exact doc, note or message it came from. Click the citation, land on the source. No invented authority.

Private by design

A dedicated, isolated model instance for your firm. Your files and prompts are never pooled with other customers and never feed a public model.

Compounding advantage

The longer your firm works in LawnchAI, the more it knows your clients and craft. Switching cost for competitors, leverage for you.

Security & data privacy

Your firm's data is yours. Full stop.

Legal data is privileged, regulated and irreplaceable. LawnchAI is built so the intelligence comes to your data — not the other way around. Nothing your firm creates is ever used to train models beyond your own.

01
A private model per firm

A dedicated, isolated AI instance for your firm alone. Your context is never pooled with, or visible to, any other customer.

02
Never trains foundation models

Your documents, prompts and outputs never feed a public or shared model. Learning stays inside your firm's private instance.

03
Encrypted end to end

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with strict key management. Privileged material is treated as privileged.

04
Granular access control

Role-based permissions decide who sees each matter, document and conversation — down to individual files.

05
Full audit trail

Every action and every agent query is logged. Know exactly who accessed what, and what the agent was asked.

06
You own your data

Export or delete everything on demand, with retention rules you control. No lock-in, no quiet data harvesting.

Our privacy promise

Your documents, prompts and outputs are never sold, never shared, and never used to train any model outside your firm's private instance. You can export or delete your data at any time.
